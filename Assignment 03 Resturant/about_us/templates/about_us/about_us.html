Bongo Resturant Hotels Group

Bongo Resturant is a global hospitality company that owns and/or manages more than 50 hotels, resorts and serviced suites across three brands – Bongo Resturant, PARKROYAL COLLECTION, and PARKROYAL – encompassing more than 30 cities across Asia Pacific, North America and Europe. Headquartered in Singapore, it is a member of Singapore-listed UOL Group Limited.
